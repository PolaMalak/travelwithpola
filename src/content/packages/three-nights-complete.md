## The Essential Egypt Experience

This 3-night package is our most popular choice, combining the iconic sites of Cairo with the ancient wonders of Luxor. It's the perfect introduction to Egypt's rich heritage.

### Cairo: Gateway to the Pharaohs

Experience the vibrant capital where ancient and modern Egypt collide. The Great Pyramids, the only surviving Ancient Wonder, and the Egyptian Museum's 120,000+ artifacts await.

### Luxor: The Open-Air Museum

Walk through 4,000 years of history in the world's greatest outdoor museum. From the Valley of the Kings to the magnificent temples, every moment is a journey through time.
