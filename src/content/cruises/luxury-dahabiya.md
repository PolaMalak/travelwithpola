## The Ultimate Nile Experience

Dahabiyas are traditional sailing boats that once carried royalty along the Nile. Our lovingly restored vessel offers the most exclusive and authentic way to cruise Egypt's legendary river.

### Why Choose a Dahabiya?

Unlike large cruise ships, dahabiyas can stop anywhere along the riverbank, accessing sites that bigger vessels cannot reach. With only 8 cabins, you'll enjoy a truly personal experience with attentive service and flexible scheduling.
