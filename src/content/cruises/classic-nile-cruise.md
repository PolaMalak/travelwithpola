## Sail Through History

There's no better way to experience ancient Egypt than sailing the same waters the pharaohs once traveled. Our Classic Nile Cruise takes you from Luxor to Aswan, stopping at the most spectacular temples along the way.

### Your Floating Palace

The MS Royal Lotus features elegantly appointed cabins, a sun deck with swimming pool, spa facilities, and gourmet Egyptian and international cuisine. Watch the timeless Nile landscape drift by from your private balcony.

### Temple Highlights

- **Karnak**: The largest religious building ever constructed
- **Valley of Kings**: Royal tombs of the New Kingdom pharaohs
- **Edfu**: The best-preserved temple in Egypt
- **Philae**: The romantic island temple of Isis
